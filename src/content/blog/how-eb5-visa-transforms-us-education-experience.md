## Understanding the EB-5 Immigration Program

The EB-5 visa is a powerful alternative to the traditional international student pathway. The program was established by the federal government in 1990 to boost the U.S. economy and provides a direct pathway to a U.S. green card through investment immigration. The EB-5 visa allows individuals already in the United States to concurrently file for their Employment authorization document (work authorization) and their Advanced Parole (Travel Authorization) when they file the initial EB-5 petition. These interim benefits are what are particularly attractive for international students and H-1B professionals.

## How EB-5 Works for International Students

The EB-5 program grants investors, their spouses and unmarried children under the age of 21 permanent residency in the United States. The requirements for the EB-5 program are the following:

- **Minimum Investment:** $800,000 in a TEA (Targeted Employment Area) or $1,050,000 in a non targeted area
- **Job Creation:** The EB 5 investment must create at least 10 new permanent full time jobs for qualified U.S. workers
- **Source of funds documentation:** The investor must document the lawful origin of investment capital
- **Investment Structure:** Funds are typically invested in real estate or business projects and are usually returned in 5 to 7 years

The key difference: EB-5 immigration is an investment, not a donation or fee. You have the potential to recover your capital while gaining permanent residency for your entire family.

## Advantages of EB-5 for Prospective Students

The EB-5 visa brings certainty to what is an unpredictable and high stress journey. An international student with a green card doesn't have to wait for student visa processing, risk consulate closure, travel and re-entry risk, or face the possibility of visa denial after receiving university admission. The green card grants permanent residency and gives the investor the same benefits and opportunities that they would have as if they were a naturalized citizen.

Many students receive admission to their dream university only to face devastating visa denials or months (sometimes year) long delays due to consulate backlogs. Some students immigration applicants in countries like India have waited over 500 days just for a visa interview appointment. The EB-5 visa eliminates the entire source of stress and uncertainty and gives the international student the freedom to explore their potential during their studies.

## Financial Advantages: Massive Tuition Savings

Understanding the U.S. tuition structure is crucial to appreciating the financial power of an EB-5 visa green card. The United States has three tiers of tuition:

1. **In-State Tuition (Lowest Cost):** For students who reside in the state where the university is located for at least 12 months. This is the most affordable type of tuition.

2. **Out of state tuition (Middle Cost):** For students who are U.S. residents but live outside the state where the university is located. Still significantly cheaper than international rates.

3. **International Student tuition (Highest Cost):** For students who are not U.S. residents. This is the most expensive way to attend a U.S. university.

With an EB-5 visa Green Card investors can save $200,000+ over four years by qualifying for in-state tuition rates. If you have a Green Card you can move to your desired state and establish residency by attending high school for a year or studying at a community college and then qualify for in-state tuition at top universities like UC Berkley, UCLA or USC.

For a family with multiple children pursing US immigration and U.S. education these savings can total $400,000 to $600,000+ and make a significant portion of your EB 5 investment self-funded through tuition savings alone.

## Federal Student Loans Through Immigration and Investment

U.S. permanent residents have access to federal student loans for education which are substantially cheaper than private international student loans.

### The Interest Rate Advantage

Federal student loans usually range from 5-7% interest rates while international student loans range from 10-11% or higher per annum in most countries. These savings can be astronomical over the course of the loan.

Access to federal loans means you can finance your education affordably without the burden of higher interest rates on private loans.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the benefits of concurrent filing with an EB-5 petition?

Concurrent filing allows the EB-5 investor to stay in the United States legally and receive interim benefits like work authorization (EAD) and travel authorization (AP) while the EB-5 petition is being processed by USCIS. This is extremely valuable for investors who are already in the United States on nonimmigrant visas.

### What happens after my EB-5 petition is approved?

After the I-526E petition is approved the investor can adjust their status. After adjustment of status approved the investor will receive conditional permanent residency for two years.

### How long will the EB-5 process take?

Processing times vary based on country of origin, current visa availability and USCIS. If the investor concurrently files the I-526E (Initial EB-5 petition), I-485 (Adjustment of Status), Employment Authorization Document and Advance Parole and the regional center has an approved I-956F the processing times can be as short as three to six months for the Work and Travel authorization, six to eight months for the I-526 approval if investing in a rural project which has priority processing and an additional six to eight months for the I-585 approval after the I-526 approval.

### What are the main financial benefits of obtaining an EB-5 visa compared to studying on an F-1 visa?

The EB-5 visa grants permanent residency which allows international students to qualify for in-state tuition (which is significantly lower than international student tuition). Additionally, green card holders have access to a broader range of scholarships, grants, federal student loans, and more favorable interest rates than private international loans. For families with multiple children, the combined savings can substantially offset the EB-5 initial investment cost.

### How does the EB-5 visa program provide greater academic flexibility for international students?

With an EB-5 green card, international students can freely choose any course or major without the restrictions imposed on F-1 visa holders. They can take gap years or transfer between institutions without risking their immigration status. This flexibility supports a more personalized and exploratory educational experience and is unbound by the strict timelines and limitations of traditional student visa.

### In what ways does the EB-5 visa improve career prospects for international students in the U.S.?

EB-5 green card holders enjoy unrestricted work authorization which makes them more attractive to employers for internships and full-time employment. This eliminates the challenges associated with the H-1B visa lottery system commonly faced by F-1 visa students after graduation and ultimately provides a more secure and straightforward path to building a career in the United States.
