The United States has consistently drawn the world's most promising young minds to its elite private boarding schools and prestigious universities. These international students are from affluent and globally minded families and represent a significant multi-year investment in human capital. These groups contribute billions of dollars to the United States economy and enrich the academic landscape every year. However, this elite cohort is often forgotten and disregarded. Their journey is overshadowed by demoralizing uncertainty represented by the shaky F-1 student visa, the volatility of Optical Practical Training and the highly competitive and restrictive H-1B visa lottery.

To combat these uncertainties families have been turning to investment-based immigration pathways, specifically the EB-5 immigrant investor program. The EB-5 program is an essential mechanism that secures the future of these students and unlocks career opportunities, financial advantages and transforms their American experience from one of temporary study to one of permanent contribution and belonging. For foreign-born students already in the U.S., our <a href="/pathways/f1-to-eb5-self-sponsored-green-card">F-1 to EB-5 green card pathway</a> lays out each step from student status to permanent residency. For more inspiring examples, read about how <a href="/research/golden-visa-global-education-student-stories">golden visas have transformed students' educational journeys</a>.

For American born private boarding school students, a permanent residency card abroad represents a similar but different journey. American students are more globally minded than ever and can use their parents EU Golden visa to apply for some of the top universities in Europe. These universities offer high quality education at a fraction of the cost compared to the universities in the United States. Many American students are drawn to the culture of Europe, the significantly lower cost of living and education, and the chance to expand their horizons and networks. Students gain access to a lifestyle that is more affordable and less stressful with endless opportunities.

## Foreign Born International Students

The Green card obtained through the EB-5 program unlocks a level of career sovereignty that is unattainable through other visas. A green card holder is free to work for any company, in any industry and anywhere in the United States without restriction. This is crucial for graduates from elite universities and boarding schools who are often targeted for high growth, high-impact sectors like tech, finance and consulting where career agility and rapid advancement are the norm.

Many high-level corporate positions require U.S. citizenship or Lawful Permanent Resident (LPR) status due to security clearance requirements. By securing a green card a foreign-born international student gains access to the full spectrum of the American job market which includes these lucrative roles.

The EB-5 green card also empowers students to pursue entrepreneurship without constraint. Graduates from top institutions often possess the innovative spirit and business acumen to launch their own ventures. With the EB-5 green card, ambitious students can establish a company, raise capital and contribute to the economy directly. This freedom to innovate and create is a massive opportunity for international students to accelerate their growth and achieve their version of the American dream.

## American Students Going Abroad

A standard student visa is a temporary document and is tied to a single institution for a specific duration of study. The golden visa offers long-term status that fundamentally changes the student's relationship with the country and the region.

One of the immediate logistical advantages of the Golden visa residency permit is the ability to bypass the complex, restricted and stressful student visa application process. A student visa typically imposes strict limitations on work hours, requires annual renewal and is contingent upon continuous enrollment. A golden visa holder is free from all of these constraints.

EU Citizens and EU permanent residents pay dramatically lower tuition rates than international students and in some cases no tuition at all. This financial disparity transforms the golden visa from a luxury investment into a cost saving measure for families planning multiple years of international education.

## The Financial Offset

The EB-5 program requires substantial investment but if done correctly can create immense savings for foreign born international students. For American students interested in studying abroad, the same principle applies. The difference between most American universities versus universities in the EU or in other countries is staggering.

For foreign international students the most immediate and quantifiable financial benefit is the ability to qualify for in-state tuition or resident tuition rates at public universities. International students typically pay tuition that is two to three times higher than that of in-state students. For example, a family with 2+ children enrolled in a private boarding school who secure an EB-5 green card before the child enters public university could save enough in tuition alone to significantly offset the initial EB-5 investment.

Green card holders gain access to federal financial aid, scholarships, and grants that are strictly unavailable to F-1 visa holders. They also benefit from the ability to secure better loans and financing because they are viewed as permanent residents with a stable future in the United States. The EB-5 should be viewed as a strategic financial instrument that unlocks the resident benefits and secures the entire family's future in the United States.

## Conclusion

### Foreign Born International Students

The choice between the uncertainty of the H-1B lottery, Optical Practical Training or student visa versus the security of the EB-5 green card is a choice between precarity and permanence. The EB-5 program offers a transformative solution that directly addresses the post-graduation crisis for foreign born international students. It provides life-changing stability by eliminating the fear of visa expiration, grants unprecedented professional and networking opportunities and delivers a powerful economic advantage by unlocking resident tuition rates. International students that leverage the EB-5 program convert their significant education investment into a secure permanent future and ensure that they have the optionality to become the best version of themselves. The EB-5 program is a strategic pathway for international students to secure their American dream.

### American Born International Students

The growing trend of American students utilizing golden visas to study abroad is a clear indicator of a strategic shift in how global education is valued and accessed. The golden visa provides financial liberation, network expansion and freedom of expression. The golden visa is a strategic investment in the future of ambitious globally minded families and their equally ambitious children.
