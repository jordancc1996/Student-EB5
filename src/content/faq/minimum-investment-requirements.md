The minimum <a href="/research/complete-2027-eb5-guide">EB-5 investment</a> is $800,000 for investments in Targeted Employment Areas (TEAs) or $1,050,000 for investments in non-TEA areas. These amounts are current as of 2025 and represent the threshold required to qualify for the <a href="/research/complete-2027-eb5-guide">EB-5 program</a>. TEAs include rural areas or regions with high unemployment rates.
