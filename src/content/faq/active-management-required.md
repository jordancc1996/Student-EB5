No, you don't need to be actively involved in day-to-day management. The EB-5 program allows for passive investment, which is one of its key advantages for busy professionals and students. You must maintain a policy-making role in the enterprise, but this doesn't require daily operational involvement. Regional center investments are particularly suitable for passive investors.
