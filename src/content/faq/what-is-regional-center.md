A Regional Center is a USCIS-designated organization that sponsors EB-5 investment projects. Regional Centers allow investors to count both direct and indirect job creation, making it easier to meet the 10-job requirement. They typically offer pre-vetted investment opportunities in real estate, infrastructure, or business development projects, making the EB-5 process more accessible for passive investors.
