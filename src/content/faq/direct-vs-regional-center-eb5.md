Direct EB-5 requires you to invest in and manage your own business, creating 10 direct jobs. Regional Center EB-5 allows investment in a project managed by the regional center, and you can count both direct and indirect jobs toward the requirement. Regional centers offer more flexibility and are typically preferred by investors who want a more passive investment approach. Direct EB-5 offers more control but requires more involvement.
